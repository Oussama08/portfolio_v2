Software Developement

Starting with basic websites few years ago I found myself working at some complex projects, the thing that motivated to get more work done.

Out of this love for problem solving and coding, my passion for aesthetic design envolved.

UI / UX Design

A user-centered mindset and sensitivity for design turned out to be the perfect fit when collaborating with some outstanding friends.

This rough framework outlines my process of developing digital experiences :

1. Research and gather the present state
2. Hot Shower
3. Structure setup and content of the project
4. Concept and Strategy
5. Create, and turn ideas into art like :

  • Carousels
  • Templates
  • Use cases and scenarios
  • User flow
  • Sketches and Infographics
  • Wireframes

To turn ideas into art and to achieve the goal of making the web a better place I trust in teamwork, latest technology and intuition.
